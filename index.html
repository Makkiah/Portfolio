Project 1: Top Ten Games



There’s no limit to the amount of games that we’ve loved booting up and getting completely immersed in, but that doesn’t quite cut it for this list. For this, we had to figure out the best of the best. Which games were so far ahead of their time, so much pure fun, that they stand apart? Since we love games we decided to answer this question once and for all. - What are the ’Top 10 Games of All Time’? This list refrences IGN’s ‘Top 100 Games of All Time’ list.
